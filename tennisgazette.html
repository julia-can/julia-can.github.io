Surfer's line up - Book cover

The cover reflects on the book’s main themes of friendship, individuality and the small coastal-town-life. Targeted at young adults who enjoy stories of self-discovery and adventure, the contemporary fiction/adventure book serves a reflective tone, rooted in coastal and surf-culture.

The design features three surfboards aligned next to each other, symbolizing the connection between the characters. The clean and bold typography conveys a contemporary and simple aesthetic, that allows the graphic to communicate its message more effectively. The white-sand-coloured background provides a calm beach backdrop vibe, that highlights the surfboards.

Presented in a bright, minimalistic and professional mockup that combines well with the beach tones, the book cover design captures the spirit of surfing-life, inviting readers to a capturing story.
